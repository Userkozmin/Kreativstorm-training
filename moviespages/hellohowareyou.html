"Hello... How Are You" is a delightful Romanian romantic comedy that embraces the quirks and complexities of modern relationships. With a touch of humor and heart, the film revolves around a woman who, after discovering her husband's affair, decides to explore the world of online dating. In a whimsical twist of fate, she connects with her husband unknowingly through an online chatroom. The movie skillfully explores the dynamics of love, forgiveness, and the unexpected paths life can take. With its witty dialogue and endearing characters, "Hello... How Are You" offers a refreshing take on love's unpredictable journey, leaving audiences with a warm and resonant feeling long after the credits roll.
